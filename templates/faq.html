BreastCareInsights is a platform designed to assist radiologists and oncologists in detecting breast cancer early by analyzing patient images like mammograms using advanced AI techniques and XAI (Explainable AI) frameworks.

Simply navigate to the home page and click the "Upload Image" button. You can upload X-rays, mammograms, or other imaging files in supported formats.

We currently support JPEG, PNG, and DICOM formats for image uploads. Other formats might be added in future updates.

The AI model analyzes the uploaded images using deep learning algorithms and provides insights regarding possible malignancies. Our Explainable AI (XAI) component ensures transparency in the decision-making process by providing detailed explanations for the predictions.

The AI model has been trained on a large dataset of breast cancer cases and has shown high accuracy in detecting early-stage cancer. However, the final decision should always be made by a certified medical professional based on clinical judgment and additional tests.
